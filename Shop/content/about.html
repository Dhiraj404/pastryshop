ABOUT US

History

Wonderland Pastries Shop was born at 2001. This shop was found and raised by Mike. But he has started selling cakes since before the Wonderland was born. He delivered the cakes whenever there was an order. Wonderland Pastries Shop became popular since 2012 when people were craving about rainbow cakes. Then Mike took that challenge to make the rainbow cake with his own way and recipe. Because of that, Wonderland Pastries won an "Favourite Pastries Shop 2012" award from government. The taste of our homemade cakes is guaranteed.
